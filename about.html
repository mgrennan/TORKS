The forcast is judged as:
Morning runs from six (6) to 12 (noon).
Afternoon from from noon to sunset.

One star (*) if the chance of precipition is less then 10%.
One star if wind will be less than or equal to ten (10) miles per hour.
One star if wind direction will be between 235 and 35 degrees (north) or 145 and 215 (south).
One star if the tempature will be between 65 and 85 degrees fahrenheit.
One star if the cloud cover will be less than 60%.
